Vision

Students are the major stakeholders of any educational instituition. The growth of the institute is therefore heavily dependent on our academic, social and emotional wellbeing. VNIT is commited to the welfare of the students and ensures that they have opportunities to grow academically, develops leadership skills and understand the core values of life.
